How to Play 🤔

  • Flip two cards to find a matching pair!
  • If they match, they stay face up ✅
  • If they don't match, they flip back 🔄
  • Find ALL the pairs to finish the round!
  • There are 5 rounds total.
  • Try to use as few flips as possible!
